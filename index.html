School of AI

Siraj Raval is an evangelist and educator of Artificial Intelligence and it is his global mission to get the word out to everyone - through AI learning communities round the globe. This gave rise to what is today known as School of AI with the vision to make AI accessible to everyone, irrespective of where they belong to.

School of AI Kochi

As a part of this initiative, Kochi is ready with a School of AI of its own. At the school of AI Kochi, we take you from the fundamentals of AI and proceed progressively towards the bigger picture that tomorrow's world requires. No matter at which stage of the progression you join in, our community is always beginner friendly thanks to the well compiled learning track to suit your convenience.
